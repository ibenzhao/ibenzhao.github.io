Southern oregon climate action now is a grassroots, volunteer,non-profit organization of area residents who care about climate change and have joined forces to take bold action against it. The design believes that everyone should respect nature, and advocates sustainable development to protect the environment. The illustrations depict the diversity of flora and fauna in the form of hand-painted, rich colors, emphasizing that people should focus on protecting the environment. The hand-painted style is immature and full of fun with childlike innocence.


At the same time, the project also emphasizes conveying the joy of young people in the form of art. At the same time, the color of the illustration is very adorable, which is very special and more easier catches audience’s attentions. While the illustrations are immature, they also form a specific visual effect, and the color matching forms a style of art.
